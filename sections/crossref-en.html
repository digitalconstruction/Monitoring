domain objectc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/DomainObject

Domain objects is a abstract concept that refers to all the central objects that are discussed in the construction project planning and management. Sub-classes of the domain object include the 'Activity','Group','Information Entity','Physical Entity',and 'Resource'. Domain objects can have their specific classification that is defined by certain classification scheme in the construction domain, such as Uniformat, Omniclass and etc. Meanwhile, they can also have their level assignment that is defined based level of detail, level of development, level of application and etc.
